Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 30, 2025

At AMOSKYS, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share your personal information when you use our Neural Security Command Platform.

By using AMOSKYS, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Password (stored as an Argon2id hash)
  • Account creation timestamp
  • Email verification status
  • Last login timestamp

1.2 Agent Telemetry Data

Our security agents collect system telemetry from devices you authorize:

Data Type Purpose Retention
Process Information Detect malicious processes 90 days
Network Activity Identify suspicious connections 90 days
File Integrity Hashes Monitor unauthorized changes 90 days
System Performance Detect resource abuse 90 days
SNMP Metrics Network device monitoring 90 days
Security Events Threat correlation 1 year

1.3 Usage Information

We automatically collect:

  • IP addresses (for rate limiting and security)
  • Browser type and version
  • API endpoint usage patterns
  • Session activity timestamps
  • Error logs and debugging information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide Security Services: Monitor deployed agents, detect threats, correlate events
  • Authenticate Users: Verify identity and manage sessions
  • Send Notifications: Email alerts for security events and account activities
  • Improve Our Platform: Enhance threat detection algorithms and AI models
  • Prevent Abuse: Rate limiting, fraud detection, and security monitoring
  • Comply with Legal Obligations: Respond to lawful requests and protect rights

3. Data Security and Encryption

Zero-Trust Security Architecture

AMOSKYS employs enterprise-grade security measures to protect your data at every layer.

3.1 Encryption

  • Data in Transit: TLS 1.3 with perfect forward secrecy
  • Data at Rest: AES-256 encryption for all stored telemetry
  • Password Storage: Argon2id hashing (OWASP 2024 recommended)
  • Agent Communication: Mutual TLS (mTLS) authentication
  • Session Tokens: SHA-256 hashed, HTTPOnly cookies

3.2 Access Controls

  • Role-based access control (RBAC) for all data
  • Multi-factor authentication (when enabled)
  • IP-based rate limiting to prevent brute-force attacks
  • Automated session expiration after inactivity
  • Audit logging of all administrative actions

3.3 Infrastructure Security

  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Isolated database environments with encrypted backups
  • Network segmentation and firewall protection
  • Real-time intrusion detection systems
  • Automated vulnerability scanning

4. Data Retention and Deletion

4.1 Retention Periods

Data Category Default Retention Notes
Account Information Until account deletion Required for service operation
Telemetry Data 90 days Configurable per organization

Audit Logs 2 years Compliance requirement
Session Data 30 days after session ends Automatically purged

4.2 Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your data:

  • Account Deletion: Request via email to privacy@amoskys.com
  • Processing Time: Complete within 30 days
  • Scope: All personal data and telemetry permanently deleted
  • Exceptions: Data required for legal compliance (audit logs) retained separately
  • Backups: Deleted from active systems; backup retention follows industry standards

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

5.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

AMOSKYS will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information or telemetry data.

5.2 Limited Sharing

We only share data in these specific circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting, email delivery (under strict DPAs)
  • Threat Intelligence: Anonymous threat indicators shared with security community
  • Legal Requirements: Court orders, subpoenas, or lawful government requests
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition (with user notification)
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing

5.3 Third-Party Services

AMOSKYS integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Cloud infrastructure providers (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
  • Email delivery services (for account verification and alerts)
  • Analytics tools (anonymized usage data only)
  • CDN providers (for static content delivery)

All third-party integrations are bound by Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) that ensure GDPR compliance.

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

6.1 GDPR Rights (EU/EEA Users)

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Right to Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Revoke consent at any time

6.2 CCPA Rights (California Users)

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to say no to the sale of personal information
  • Right to access your personal information
  • Right to equal service and price
